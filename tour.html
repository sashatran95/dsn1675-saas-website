Our Purpose

Animating is not an easy and cheap skill to achieve, so we will help you out by giving you access to this free and easy to access opportunity to do so. That’s when we designed an online web animation service that allows users to upload their logos and select pre-made animations that can be customized using sliders, then exported/downloaded as a web ready .swf file.


MyMotion is streamlined logo manipulation software that works remotely to create world-class logo animation to anyone, everywhere. The primary target audience is towards people and businesses that cannot afford an on-hand back-end designer to take their website to the next level with professional design and motions.


People who are just starting up or want to spice up their website will gravitate to the easy-to-use software that goes hand in hand with products like Shopify and Square Space. Mom and pop business or local designers that want to have a professional website on a budget can sign up for MyMotion to have their website transformed.
