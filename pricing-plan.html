Pricing

Freemium

The freemium option allows the user to upload their logo/image, and manipulate the animation however they want using the native controls and download the finished animation for free in low res.

One-Time Purchase

The one time purchase allows the user to upload their logo/image and manipulate the animation however they want using the native controls and go through the whole process for free up to the point of purchase. They can purchase the individual .swf file they created. Once purchased/downloaded the project is deleted and they have no method to go back and edit the project without restarting the one time purchase process or purchasing a membership.

Membership Purchase

The membership purchase is a yearly fixed price that allows the user to create and save their projects to their profile. It also allows them to go back to their profile and edit any created project. It also allows for unlimited downloads and unlimited project creations.
